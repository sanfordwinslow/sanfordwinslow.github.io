Welcome To My Life of Adventures

Hello! My name is Sanford Winslow. I am a native Washingtonian and grew up in the Seattle area. I have traveled and lived in many parts of the world but the beautiful Pacific Northwest keeps me coming back. I am avid outdoors person who enjoys spending every available opportunity enjoying some outdoor adventure.

I got my first taste of the adventure when my friends took me rock climbing when I was in college. That's when I was hooked. I can still remember the thrill and the excitement of my first climb. It wasn't long after I got my first snowboard and I was screaming down mountainsides. My friends and I have enjoyed countless adventures together in the outdoors pursuing what we enjoy and love to do. Over the years, I have tried many different activities and am thankful to those friends who introduced me so many years ago.
